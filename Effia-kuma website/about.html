About Effia-Kuma Musama Church International

Effia-Kuma Musama Church International is a vibrant Christian community committed to spreading the message of love, faith, and service. The church nurtures strong relationships with God, promotes moral excellence, and encourages practical expressions of faith through outreach and social programs.

Members grow spiritually through engaging worship services, Bible studies, prayer sessions, and discipleship programs. The church emphasizes leadership development, youth engagement, and talent utilization in music, drama, media, and ministry, fostering a culture of active participation and service.

Mission: To transform lives through the power of God’s Word, prayer, and community service.
Vision: To be a Christ-centered church that nurtures believers, strengthens communities, and impacts the world with God’s love.
