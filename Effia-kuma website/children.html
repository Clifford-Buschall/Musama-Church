Children Ministry

The Children Ministry at Effia-Kuma Musama Church International is dedicated to nurturing young hearts in the ways of the Lord. Our mission is to provide a fun, safe, and loving environment where children can learn about God, develop good morals, and grow spiritually.

Through interactive Bible lessons, songs, games, arts & crafts, and storytelling, children are encouraged to explore their faith and talents. We aim to equip every child with the knowledge of God’s Word and the skills to live a Christ-centered life from a young age.
