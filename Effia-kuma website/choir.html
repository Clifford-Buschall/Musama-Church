About the Choir

The Musama Church international (MCI) Effia-Kuma, was founded in the early 1920s, developed its choir as part of the church’s organizational structure when worship groups and societies were formalized in the 1930s. The choir was created to support worship through music, enhance church services, and represent the church during gatherings and major conventions.

Over time, it became an important part of the church’s identity expressing Christian faith through African cultural musical styles. Today, the choir operates across various branches and continues to play a key role in worship, community life, and church ceremonies.
