Effia-Kuma Musama Youth

The Effia-Kuma Musama Youth is a vibrant fellowship focused on spiritual growth, discipline, and active participation in church activities. The group empowers young people through Bible studies, mentorship, leadership training, social programs, and community outreach. Members use their talents in music, evangelism, media, drama, ushering, and other departments, promoting unity and a passion for God’s work.
